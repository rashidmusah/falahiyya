Our Academic Philosophy

Integrating Arabic and English education for global citizenship

At Falahiyya Arabic and English Educational Complex, we've developed a unique bilingual curriculum that seamlessly integrates Ghana's national curriculum with comprehensive Arabic and Islamic studies. Our approach develops students who are not only academically proficient but also culturally literate in both Western and Islamic traditions.

Our academic program is designed around three core pillars:

  • Academic Excellence in both Arabic and English mediums
  • Character Development grounded in Islamic values
  • Global Citizenship through cultural understanding

With a student-teacher ratio of 15:1 and specialized language instruction, we ensure personalized attention and optimal learning outcomes for all students.

Academic Programs

Comprehensive educational pathways from kindergarten to senior high

Bilingual Immersion

Daily instruction in both Arabic and English with native-speaking teachers. Focus on foundational language skills through play-based learning.

  • Arabic phonics and vocabulary
  • English pre-reading skills
  • Storytelling in both languages

Islamic Foundation

Introduction to Islamic values, Quranic recitation, and basic Islamic practices through stories, songs, and activities.

  • Memorization of short surahs
  • Islamic manners and etiquette
  • Introduction to prayer

Early Development

Developmentally appropriate activities to build cognitive, social, and motor skills. Focus on creativity and exploration.

  • Sensory play stations
  • Math and science exploration
  • Art and music activities

Core Subjects

Mathematics, Science, English Language Arts, and Social Studies taught in English. Arabic Language and Islamic Studies taught in Arabic.

  • Daily Arabic language instruction
  • STEM exploration activities
  • Project-based learning

Quranic Studies

Memorization of Juz Amma, proper Tajweed, and understanding of basic Quranic concepts. Daily Quran recitation practice.

  • Tajweed rules application
  • Quranic vocabulary building
  • Meaningful memorization techniques

Technology Integration

Introduction to computer literacy, educational software, and basic coding concepts. Computer lab access twice weekly.

  • Digital citizenship
  • Introduction to programming
  • Educational apps for language learning

STEM Focus

Enhanced science and mathematics curriculum with hands-on experiments and project-based learning. Preparation for BECE.

  • Science laboratory experiments
  • Math olympiad preparation
  • Robotics and coding clubs

Arabic Literature

Advanced Arabic language studies including grammar, composition, and classical Arabic literature analysis.

  • Classical poetry studies
  • Modern Arabic literature
  • Creative writing in Arabic

Leadership Program

Development of leadership skills through student government, community service projects, and peer mentoring.

  • Student council
  • Community outreach
  • Public speaking training

Academic Tracks

Specialized programs in General Arts, Business, Science, and Islamic Studies. Preparation for WASSCE and university admission.

  • Advanced science courses
  • Business management
  • Islamic jurisprudence

Global Perspectives

Courses on world history, international relations, and comparative religion with focus on Islamic civilizations.

  • Model United Nations
  • Cultural exchange programs
  • Current affairs analysis

Career Preparation

Internship opportunities, career counseling, and university guidance. Entrepreneurship and vocational skills development.

  • University application support
  • Career exploration workshops
  • Entrepreneurship training

Our Unique Curriculum

Balancing Ghana's national standards with Arabic and Islamic education

Arabic & Islamic Studies

  • Quranic Studies: Memorization, Tajweed, and Tafsir
  • Arabic Language: Modern Standard Arabic with focus on reading, writing, and conversation
  • Islamic History: Prophetic biography and Islamic civilization
  • Fiqh & Aqeedah: Islamic jurisprudence and theology
  • Islamic Ethics: Character development based on Quran and Sunnah
  • Arabic Literature: Classical and contemporary works

English & General Studies

  • Core Subjects: Mathematics, Science, English, Social Studies
  • ICT Education: Computer literacy and programming fundamentals
  • Creative Arts: Music, visual arts, and drama
  • Physical Education: Sports and health education
  • French Language: West African regional language
  • Research Skills: Critical thinking and academic writing

Begin Your Educational Journey

Join our vibrant community of learners and achievers

At Falahiyya Arabic and English Educational Complex, we welcome students from diverse backgrounds to experience our unique bilingual education program. Our admissions process is designed to identify students who will thrive in our academic environment while contributing positively to our school community.

We accept applications year-round for Kindergarten through Senior High School. Boarding facilities are available for students from Grade 4 and above.

Our next academic term begins on September 5, 2025. The deadline for applications is July 31, 2026.

Admission Process

Simple steps to join our school community

1

Submit Application

Complete the online application form and pay the non-refundable application fee of GH₵ 100

2

Schedule Assessment

Our admissions office will contact you to schedule an entrance assessment and interview

3

Submit Documents

Provide required documents including previous school records and birth certificate

4

Receive Decision

Admissions decisions are communicated within 2 weeks of completing all requirements

Admission Requirements

What you need to apply to our programs

Kindergarten

  • Child must be 4 years old by September 1
  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate
  • Immunization records
  • Recent passport photograph

Primary School

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate
  • Previous school reports (2 years)
  • Recommendation letter from previous school
  • Pass entrance assessment

Junior High

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate
  • All previous school reports
  • Pass entrance examination and interview

Scholarships & Financial Aid

Investing in exceptional students

Merit-Based Scholarships

Falahiyya offers several scholarship opportunities for outstanding students who demonstrate academic excellence, leadership potential, and strong moral character.

Need-Based Financial Aid

We believe financial circumstances should not prevent qualified students from accessing quality education. Limited need-based aid is available for families who qualify.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum B+ average in previous school
  • Excellent performance on entrance exam
  • Demonstrated leadership qualities
  • Strong recommendation letters
  • Financial need documentation (for need-based aid)

Our Story

Established in 2021, Falahiyya Arabic and English Educational Complex began as a small community initiative to provide quality bilingual education in Northern Ghana. What started with just 35 students in two classrooms has grown into a premier educational institution serving over 200+ students from kindergarten through junior high school.

Named after the Arabic word for "successful" or "prosperous", Falahiyya has lived up to its name by consistently producing graduates who excel academically while maintaining strong moral character grounded in Islamic values.

Over the past three years, we've expanded our facilities, developed specialized bilingual curricula, and created a unique boarding program that nurtures students academically, spiritually, and socially.

Our Mission

To provide a comprehensive bilingual education that develops students intellectually, morally, and spiritually, preparing them to excel in both Arabic and English academic environments while becoming responsible global citizens.

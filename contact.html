Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our school and admissions process

What are the school hours?

Our regular school hours are from 7:30 AM to 3:00 PM, Monday to Friday. Boarding students have structured evening study sessions from 6:00 PM to 8:00 PM. Weekend activities are organized for boarding students.

How do I apply for admission?

Admission applications can be submitted online through our website or in person at the school administration office. You'll need to provide previous school records, birth certificate, and passport photos. All applicants take an entrance assessment test.

What is the student-teacher ratio?

We maintain a low student-teacher ratio of 15:1 to ensure personalized attention. In specialized language classes, the ratio is even lower at 12:1 to maximize learning effectiveness.

Are scholarships available?

Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships for outstanding students and need-based financial aid. Scholarships cover up to 75% of tuition fees. Applications are reviewed quarterly by our scholarship committee.

Can parents visit boarding facilities?

Absolutely! We encourage prospective parents to tour our boarding facilities. Tours are scheduled every Tuesday and Thursday between 10:00 AM and 2:00 PM. Please contact our boarding office to arrange a visit.

About Falahiyya

Established in 2021, Falahiyya Arabic and English Educational Complex provides quality bilingual education with boarding facilities for students from kindergarten to senior high school.
